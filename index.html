Pyramid Equation Game

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

A mathematical puzzle game that challenges both memory and problem-solving skills.

Memorize Phase (10 seconds):

  • Study the pyramid of hexagonal tiles (labeled A to J)
  • Each tile displays a number or operation (e.g., +5, ×2, -3)
  • Remember the target number displayed at the top

    • Solve Phase:

  • Tiles flip to hide their content, showing only their labels
  • Click on 2-4 tiles in sequence to form an equation
  • If your equation equals the target → score +1
  • If incorrect, tiles flip back for another attempt

    • Game Progression:

  • 5 rounds with increasing difficulty
  • Beautiful Ghibli-inspired visual feedback
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible!
